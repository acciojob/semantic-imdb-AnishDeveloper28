Movie Summary

A mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in the art of extracting secrets from within people's dreams. Given a chance to erase his criminal past, Cobb must accomplish the impossible: planting an idea deep within a target’s subconscious. As the dream world collapses around him, reality blurs, leading to an unforgettable journey.
